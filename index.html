How to buy

  • 1

    Add SUI to your wallet

    Buy SUI on a CEX and withdraw to a Sui-compatible wallet (e.g., Phantom (Sui), Sui Wallet, Nightly, OKX).

  • 2

    Connect your wallet on blast.fun

    Open blast.fun → “Connect Wallet” and authorize your Sui wallet.

  • 3

    Find $KAPPA

    Use search on blast.fun and paste the verified contract address for $KAPPA to avoid fakes. (You’ll see token states like NEW / BONDING / BONDED on the page.)

  • 4

    Swap SUI → $KAPPA

    On the $KAPPA page, enter the SUI amount, set slippage if needed, and Confirm. If it’s still on the bonding curve, press Buy; if it’s migrated, use the trade button shown there.
